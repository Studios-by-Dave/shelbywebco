Cities & Municipalities to Target

The opportunity: Cleveland County, with Shelby as the county seat, hosts nearly 100,000 residents and a diverse economic base — advanced manufacturing, healthcare, aerospace & defense, textiles, and small retail. Most local businesses have either no website or an aging, non-mobile-friendly one. Competition for local web design search terms is thin: results are dominated by national aggregator pages (Thumbtack, Yelp, Clutch) and a handful of loosely optimized local agencies. A tightly structured local SEO campaign can dominate the first page for dozens of high-intent keywords within 6–12 months.

The buyer: A small-business owner in Shelby or Kings Mountain who is ready to hire — not browse. They are searching with urgency words ("hire," "near me," "affordable," "quote") and location modifiers. These are not researchers; they are buyers with a budget. Your content strategy must meet them where they are.

Section 7

Technical & Google Business Profile Notes

Schema Markup Priorities

Implement these schema types on every relevant page to appear in rich results and AI-generated answers:

LocalBusiness schema (all pages) ProfessionalService schema (homepage) FAQPage schema (pricing, process, FAQ pages) Review / AggregateRating schema BreadcrumbList schema (all pages) ServiceArea schema (list all target towns)

Google Business Profile Optimization

NAP Consistency: Your business Name, Address, and Phone must be identical across your website, GBP, Yelp, BBB, and every other directory. Even minor variations ("St." vs "Street") hurt local rankings.

Category selection: Primary category = "Web Designer." Add secondary categories: "Website Designer," "Internet Marketing Service," "Graphic Designer."

GBP Posts: Post at least twice per month featuring completed local projects. Tag the city/town. These posts appear directly in Google Search results for branded and local queries.

Review strategy: Request reviews from every completed client and respond to all of them. Reviews are one of the top 3 ranking factors for the Google Maps local pack.
